About

Genshin Impact has been one of my favorite RPG games that I've been following up with for years. In every month, the game's version keeps updating, and more characters are released, with each one having their own unique story. Of course, the game has its own main story but the story won't end until years later.

The images displayed above showcase the characters known as Archons in this game, each possessing unique elemental abilities surpassing those of other characters. It is advisable to explore their character demos on YouTube to witness not only their formidable power but also to experience the exceptional quality of their theme music. It's worth noting that these pictures are unofficial and are solely computer-drawn creations by enthusiastic fans of the game.
