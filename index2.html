About

The images displayed above feature the two protagonists of this game. Players can choose either Lumine (on the left) or her sibling Aether (on the right) as the Traveler, while the Traveler's sibling is kidnapped. With their powers sealed, they are left stranded in Teyvat. Alongside Paimon, the Traveler's new companion, they embark on a journey across Teyvat to reunite with the Traveler's sibling.

In Teyvat, the Traveler has the ability to resonate with any Statue of the Seven to obtain elemental power matching the statue's element.

Upcoming Character

Dainsleif, an eagerly anticipated playable character in Genshin Impact, initially appeared as a non-playable character in Chapter I: ACT IV - Prelude. On September 27, 2020, it was officially announced that he would become playable in the future, although the exact release date remains uncertain. In the game's lore, Dainsleif holds a compelling backstory as a former knight captain of the Royal Guard in Khaenri'ah. Unfortunately, he could not prevent the catastrophe that befell Khaenri'ah 500 years ago. As a consequence of those tragic events, Dainsleif was cursed with immortality, condemning him to live eternally in the world of Teyvat.
